Welcome to the Electricity Billing System

Efficiently manage electricity services, billing, payments, and support.

Users

Customers can create accounts, view billing information, make payments, and provide feedback.

Utility Providers manage meter readings, adjust tariffs, generate accurate bills, and handle overdue payments.

Support Administrators assist customers by resolving billing and payment issues, reviewing feedback, and improving the system.

Staff track overdue bills, analyze usage trends, and maintain customer account records.

Modules

Customer Portal: View your billing history, make payments, and manage your account.

Utility Provider Dashboard: Update meter readings, adjust tariffs, generate monthly bills, and handle overdue payments.

Support Center: Resolve issues, analyze customer feedback, and improve service quality.

Staff Management: Track overdue payments, monitor usage patterns, and maintain accurate customer records.

Getting Started

If you are a new user, you can sign up and create your account.

If you already have an account, log in to access your dashboard.
